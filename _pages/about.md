I am an AI/ML Engineer at [Polygraf AI](https://polygraf.ai), where my work spans the breadth of AI governance — from detecting AI-generated, synthetic, and manipulated content, to building privacy-preserving systems that prevent the leakage of sensitive data through AI channels.

Prior to that, I received my Ph.D. from the [Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering](https://ece.utexas.edu) at [The University of Texas at Austin](https://www.utexas.edu), where I was part of the [Wireless Networking and Communications Group (WNCG)](https://wncg.org). During my Ph.D., I worked on the design and analysis of approximation algorithms with provable performance guarantees, with a special focus on applications in energy systems.
